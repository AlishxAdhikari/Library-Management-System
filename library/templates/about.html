About Us

Welcome to Book Record Management, your digital solution for managing and organizing books efficiently. Our goal is to simplify book cataloging for libraries, schools, and book enthusiasts. We believe in clean design, fast access, and a hassle-free interface that anyone can use.

This project is built with Django and Bootstrap, aiming to provide a full-stack CRUD experience. Whether you're a librarian or a developer learning Django, this system will serve you well!
